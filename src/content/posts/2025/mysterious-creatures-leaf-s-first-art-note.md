_Beyond Writing Code - Art 1_

_June 27, 2025_

Overwhelmed? Too much email unread in your inbox? Good news: this one's mostly pictures.

I promised the occasional glimpse of my art...

I make art for [Art-o-mat](https://www.artomat.org/), which converts old cigarette vending machines to sell art. I draw mysterious creatures on 2" x 3" watercolor paper.

![](/images/posts/2025/mysterious-creatures-leaf-s-first-art-note-1.jpg)

"looking for..."

I glue each drawing to a wooden block, paint the edges, and number and name each on the back

![](/images/posts/2025/mysterious-creatures-leaf-s-first-art-note-2.jpg)

Mysterious Creatures series 6

I just sent Art-o-mat my ninth set of 50 pieces to distribute. I've drawn many more than that.

Why mysterious? Well... they do defy expectations at times:
